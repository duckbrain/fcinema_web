Installation

After downloading the latest version for Windows, or MacOS, double-click the downloaded file and follow the steps. Note that, as fCinema is not a certified Windows developer, nor it has been download from the Apple Mac App Store, some security warnings may arise. Cost of those licenses are high for our current budget, so please make a donation to help us.

Using fcinema

Basic

Watching a film in fCinema is very easy. Just open the app and search the movie you want, choose the scenes that are going to be skipped and select your favourite content provider, movie file, or DVD.

Note that you can have your favorite settings saved, and quick shortcuts to IMDB's parent's guide and Kids in mind are available within the app.

Advanced

In the settings menu, toggle editor mode. Know, while you are watching a film and if you detect an inappropriate scene, click the icon in the upper right corner and start a new film cut. Those edits are saved locally, but you can reach us to become an editor and share your scenes with the world.
